Voilega — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23.06.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Voilega (the "App"), provided by the individual developer Hikmet Elmas ("we"), collects, uses and protects your personal data. Voilega lets you listen to public-domain children's tales narrated in an AI-generated voice of your loved ones.

1. Data We Collect

Account information

  • Name (display name), email address and password. Passwords are stored hashed by our authentication provider; we never see your plain-text password.
  • Optional profile photo (if you upload one).

Voice recordings and generated voice (important)

  • When you add a "voice", you record a few short passages read by a person who consented. These recordings are sent to our voice-technology provider ElevenLabs to create a synthetic voice model (a "voice clone"), where they are processed and stored.
  • The generated voice model and the narration files produced with it are stored and linked to your account.
  • A person's voice may be considered sensitive/biometric data under some laws. We therefore ask you to confirm you have explicit permission from the person whose voice you record. We use voices only to narrate stories for you; we never share or sell them.

Usage and subscription information

  • Listening activity (which stories you complete, total listening time) — used for in-app statistics.
  • Subscription status (free/premium, plan type, renewal date). Payments are processed by Apple; we never see or store your card/bank details. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status.
  • Limited technical logs needed to operate the App.

2. How We Use Data

  • To create your account and keep you signed in.
  • To narrate stories in the voice you added (core service).
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To show in-app statistics and improve the service.
  • For security, fraud prevention and legal obligations.

We do not show ads, we do not sell your data for advertising, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools (e.g. crash or usage tracking).

3. Service Providers We Share Data With

To provide the service we share data, only as necessary, with these trusted providers:

  • Supabase — authentication, database and file storage (account, voice models, audio files).
  • ElevenLabs — voice cloning and text-to-speech (raw voice recordings are processed and stored here).
  • RevenueCat — subscription status management.
  • Apple — in-app purchases and payment processing.

These providers' servers may be located abroad (e.g. US/EU); your data may be transferred internationally, and we make reasonable efforts to ensure appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

4. Retention and Deletion

  • We keep your data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service.
  • Raw voice recordings are stored by ElevenLabs to create and maintain the voice model.
  • When you delete a voice in the App, we delete the related voice model (clone) and the narrations made with it.
  • To request deletion of your account and all related data (including data held by providers), contact us at info@voilega.com.

5. Children's Privacy

Voilega is designed to be used by parents/guardians (adults) to play stories for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country). If you believe we have inadvertently received a child's data, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures (encrypted connections, access controls, etc.) to protect your data. However, no transmission or storage over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict and object to the processing of your data. As the developer is established in the European Union (Spain), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies; for users in Türkiye, Law No. 6698 (KVKK) may apply. To exercise your rights, write to info@voilega.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. For material changes we will notify you in the App or on this page. The latest version is always available here.
