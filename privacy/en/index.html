Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Voilega mobile application (the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. Voilega narrates public-domain children's fairy tales in the AI-cloned voice of someone your family loves.

Data controller: Hikmet Elmas (individual developer)
Address: Travessera de Dalt 6 BjA, 08024 Barcelona, Spain
Contact: info@voilega.com

1. Data We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following:

  • Account information: the email address and name you provide when you register.
  • Voice recordings: the voice samples you record to clone a voice, and the voice model generated from them. Voice generation is performed through our service provider ElevenLabs.
  • In-app preferences and progress: data needed for the App to work, such as language preference, the voices you create, listening progress, and favorites.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active Premium subscription. Payments are handled by the Apple App Store and RevenueCat; we never see or store your card/payment details.

No ads, no tracking. Voilega does not show ads, does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools, and does not sell your data for marketing.

2. How We Use Data

  • To create your account and manage your session.
  • To generate a voice model from your recording and narrate stories in that voice.
  • To remember your listening progress, favorites, and language preference.
  • To manage Premium subscription access.
  • To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service.

3. Important Note About Voice Recordings

The raw voice samples you record for cloning are sent to ElevenLabs to build the voice model and may be stored by ElevenLabs on their systems. You should only create a voice using the voice of a person who is you, or someone whose explicit consent you have obtained. When you delete a voice from within the App, we make reasonable efforts to remove the associated voice model.

4. Data Sharing and Service Providers

We share your data only with the service providers necessary to operate the App:

  • Supabase — account, content, and file hosting (authentication, database, storage).
  • ElevenLabs — voice cloning and narration.
  • RevenueCat & Apple — subscription management and payment.

These providers process data only to provide services to us. We do not share your data with other third parties unless required by law.

5. Legal Basis

Under the GDPR (applicable in the EU / Spain) and the KVKK (applicable in Türkiye), we process your data based on: performance of a contract (to provide the service), your explicit consent (especially for voice recordings), and legitimate interest (to keep the service secure).

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account or your data, we remove the relevant records from our systems within a reasonable period. For voice data held by ElevenLabs, that provider's retention terms also apply.

7. Children's Privacy

Voilega is a family app and the stories are designed for children; however, accounts are created and managed by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. A child's voice should only be recorded and managed by you as their parent/legal guardian. If you believe a child's data has been processed without your consent, contact us at info@voilega.com and we will take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and KVKK, you have the right to access your data, request its correction or deletion, restrict its processing, and withdraw your consent. To exercise these rights, write to info@voilega.com. You can also delete your account and related data from within the App.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data (encrypted connections, access controls, etc.). While no system is 100% secure, we follow industry-standard practices to safeguard your data.

10. International Data Transfers

Our service providers may process data on servers located in different countries. We take care that such transfers are carried out in line with applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through the App or our website. The current version is always published on this page.
